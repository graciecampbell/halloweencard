Ghostface

Ghostface is the villain of the Scream series.

Costume: A white mask with black eyes, mouth and nose, and a black hooded robe with tattered arms that goes down to cover the feet.

Equipment:: Fake knife, fake blood, voice changer

Michael Myers

Michael Myers is the main villain of the Halloween franchise

Costume: Mechanic jumpsuit (black, grey, blue), white mask, and black work boots

Equipment: Fake kitchen knife, fake blood

Wendy Torrance

Wendy Torrance is one of the main characters of The Shining

Costume: Green plaid shirt, beige knit turtleneck, brown overall dress, brown ankle boots

Hair and Makeup:Wendy has shoulder length black hair. Use brown eyeshadow to accentuate the eyes.

Equipment:Baseball bat, fake knife

Jason Voorheese

Jason Voorhees is the main villain of the Friday the 13th franchise.

Costume: Blue tattered tee shirt, brown tattered jacket, grey/blue pants, black work boots and a hockey-esque mask.

Equipment: Fake blood, fake machete, fake axe

Clarice Starling

Clarice Starling is one of the main characters of The Silence of the Lambs.

Costume: Forest green duffle coat, brown slacks, black boots, blue scarf

Equipment:Police badge, prop gun, briefcase

Carrie White

Carrie White is the main character of Stephen King's Carrie

Costume: Light pink/white prom dress, white heels, tiara

Equipment:fake blood, bouquet of flowers
